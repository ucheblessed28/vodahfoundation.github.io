Let's Find Out

Introduction:
Let’s Find Out is a multifaceted brand activation event across Nigeria; its major objective is to serve as a Research and Development initiative aimed at finding out what people generally really do understand about Domestic Violence/ Abuse through the deployment of a (well-tailored and simple to answer ) questionnaire

Objectives:

  • Assessment of people’s understanding of what domestic violence/abuse means to them using that to create a comprehensive statistical analysis of the mindset of an average Nigerian on this issues.
  • Educate and enlighten our public on what domestic violence/abuse really is and what to do in case they find themselves in such situations or they know someone who is.
  • The event is to also serve as an Awareness/Meet & Greet platform for Help for Victims of Domestic Violence and its Volunteering members in the organizing respective State.

Men Together

The quarterly support group is for all men at RISK of Relationship Abuse AND All those who wish to support them or improve their situation. We offer practical one-one support and assistance to men and their children experiencing intimate partner abuse

We offer a non-judgmental support to reduce the disadvantage of male victims of domestic abuse facing the civil and family justice system without a paid lawyer, enabling them to access justice. We give men the space and time to share their stories and experiences and to gain support from each other who have similar experiences.

The Basement Project Recovery Program has been designed by people who have been affected by alcohol and drug issues, with the aim of helping others attain a lifestyle free from addiction, abuse, and the fear. The program aim is to help you get clean from alcohol and/or drugs and for you to understand more about the nature of the addictive illness. With this understanding, you will be able to manage a life free from addiction.

Women Together

Similar to the Men Together initiative, Women Together is a quarterly support group for all women at RISK of Relationship Abuse AND All those who wish to support them or improve their situation.

We offer practical one-one support and assistance to women and their children experiencing intimate partner abuse.

Also, we showcase voluntary testimonials from women who have suffered similar abuse in the past – to encourage those who are currently being abused speak out, and get much-required HELP.
